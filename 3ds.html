The 3DS' 2 screens are differing ratios, with the top being a wider, 5:3 aspect ratio and the bottom being in 4:3. For the record, 5:3 multiplied by 3 is 15:9, so in actuality, it isn't too sinister to use a layout or display method for a 16:9 game, which results in just a small amount of black bars on the side for the top screen. Generally it's reccomended for DS games that when streamed, you have the top screen bigger than the bottom and have the bottom on the side.
