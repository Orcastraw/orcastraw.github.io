We are returning to the San Jose Convention Center and continue the concert-style Sunday top 8 viewing at the Civic Center across the street! Come see why Genesis 3 was instantly regarded as one of the best tournaments of all time!

The Tournament Experience

The Genesis tournament series prides itself on a few standout features

  • Experienced Staff - With experienced TO staff like Bear and Dr. Z behind the event, players can expect a great and organized bracket.

  • Finals in a Theatre - Genesis Events finish out in stylish fashion, having their final top8 days take place in a decked out theatre.

  • Location - With the event taking place in sunny San Jose, there's plenty to see and do around the area.

  • Fun for players of all levels - The venue will have lots of setups

Main Venue

The main event will be held at at the San Jose Convention Center with projectors for spectating, a large stage, plenty of friendly setups, and vendors as well.
